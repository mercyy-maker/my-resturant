ABOUT US

Mesob is a family owned restaurant bringing you the beautiful Ethiopian cultural experience with our delicious food. If you haven`t tried Ethiopian food, now is the time to come to Mesob. Our food is freshly made with traditional species. Ethiopian dishes are prepared with a distinctive variety of spices for an unforgettably striking dimension of African cookery. Come in and enjoy a satisfyingly unique and exciting dining experience. We serve our famour Ethiopian coffee ceremony. It is one of the most enjoyable events you can attend at an Ethiopian Restaurant. You’ll see the coffee taken through its full life cycle during this community-rich ceremony. Ethiopians refer to coffee as ‘Bunna’ (boo-na).
